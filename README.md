# ✊✋✌️ HandOff — Rock Paper Scissors with Hand Gestures

**Human VS Machine. No clicks. Just hands.**

HandOff is a gesture-controlled Rock Paper Scissors game where you play against an AI using your **real hand via webcam** — no buttons, just show your move to the camera.

---

## 🎮 Features

- **Gesture Detection** — Uses your camera to read Rock, Paper, or Scissors in real time
- **Human VS CPU** — Face off against a randomized CPU opponent each round
- **Persistent Accounts** — Sign in once; your match history and career record are saved on the backend
- **Career Stats** — Track your Wins, Losses, and Draws across all sessions
- **Retro Arena UI** — Arcade-inspired interface built for the fight

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Open the app and click **Enter Arena**
2. Sign in (first sign-in automatically creates your account)
3. Hit **Start Round** — the camera initializes and reads your hand gesture
4. Show **✊ Rock**, **✋ Paper**, or **✌️ Scissors** to the camera
5. CPU reveals its hand — winner takes the round!

> **Camera required.** Allow browser camera access when prompted.
